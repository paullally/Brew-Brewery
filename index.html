Our Story

Welcome to Brew Brewery, your number one source for all things craft beer. We're dedicated to giving you the very best craft brews possible with a focus on flavour, diversity and price Founded in 2021 by Paul, Brew Brewery has come a long way from its beginnings as a home brewery. Brew Brewery had been a long-planned project of founder/owner/brewer Paul. Having been obsessed with beer and heavily involved in homebrewing for all his adult life, it was always an ambition to open a brewery or have a commercial brewing project that would allow him to produce a diverse range of beer. What began as a series of late-night kitchen table discussions is now a state-of-the-art brewery, producing an extensive range of premium craft beers.

Our Products

Our Process

At Brew brewery we keep our brewing process quite simple. we mill our grain then add the milled grain to hot water this is now called mash. The mash is then pumped into the lauter tun, where a sweet liquid known as wort is separated from the grain husks. We then boil the wortAfter boiling, the wort is transferred into a whirlpool for the wort separation stage. During this stage, any malt or hop particles are removed to leave a liquid that is ready to be cooled and fermented. After reaching its full potential, the beer is filtered, carbonated, and transferred to the bright beer tank, where it goes through a cellaring process that takes 3-4 weeks to complete.
